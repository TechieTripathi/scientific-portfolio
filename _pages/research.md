My research sits at the intersection of **Large Language Models**, **Knowledge Systems**, **Computer Vision**, and **AI for Healthcare**. I focus on work that is both theoretically grounded and deployable in real-world contexts.

---

## Research Areas

**Document Intelligence & OCR**
Developing and evaluating OCR pipelines for historical and archival documents — addressing degraded text, noisy scans, skewed pages, and complex layouts. My work includes fine-tuning models on low-quality inputs and building preprocessing pipelines that significantly reduce manual correction effort.

**Knowledge Graphs & LLMs**
Building systems that extract structured knowledge from unstructured text using Large Language Models. This includes entity recognition, relationship extraction, graph visualisation, and integration with RAG-based chatbots for context-aware querying.

**Machine-Generated Text Detection**
Developing classifiers to distinguish machine-generated from human-written content. Competed in the COLING 2025 GenAI Detection Task, achieving competitive results and publishing methodology findings.

**Narrative Similarity & Reasoning**
Designing structured prompting strategies for LLMs to solve narrative similarity tasks. Achieved **75% test accuracy** in SemEval 2026 Task 4, **ranking 3rd out of 47 international systems** — approaching the human annotator ceiling of 78%.

**AI for Healthcare**
Applying deep learning and NLP to mental health and healthcare challenges — including a BERT-based depression detection framework with SHAP-based explainability, and generative AI systems for patient engagement and virtual health assistance.

**Computer Vision**
Developing real-time facial recognition systems for large-scale deployment. Implemented ResNet50 + FaceNet512 pipelines for classroom attendance, validated at ICFAI University with 100% recognition accuracy on 60 identities.
