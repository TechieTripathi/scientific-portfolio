I'm **Vishnu Tripathi**, a researcher and engineer based in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, India. I specialize in applied AI — building systems that work in the real world while contributing to the frontier of knowledge through peer-reviewed research.

## Background

I completed my **Master of Computer Application (Data Science)** and **Bachelor of Computer Application** from Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya, Haridwar, graduating with a cumulative GPA of 8.85 and 8.22 respectively.

During my studies, I was concurrently active as a **Research Intern** at the CAIR Lab, as a **Full-Stack Developer** at Sensekriti Innovation, and as a participant in international AI competitions. This multi-track experience gave me an unusually broad and practical foundation — in research methodology, system architecture, and product delivery.

## What I Do

My work sits at the intersection of three areas:

**Research** — I design and validate novel AI/ML methodologies, publish findings at international conferences, and contribute to applied research in document intelligence, LLMs, and healthcare AI.

**Engineering** — I build production systems end-to-end: from backend APIs and cloud infrastructure, to deep learning pipelines and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) chatbots.

**Leadership** — I lead cross-functional research teams, run stakeholder reviews, manage project timelines, and present at international venues as a primary author.

## Current Role

I am currently a **Junior Research Fellow (JRF)** at the CAIR Lab, Institute of Artificial Intelligence, DSVV, where I lead the development of an OCR and digitisation system for historical books and manage research publication pipelines.
